Baked cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups crushed biscuits
  • 50g ground almonds
  • 70g melted butter
  • 675g cream cheese (30%)
  • 250g quark cheese (5%)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 400g sour cream (for topping)
  • ⅓ cup sugar (for topping)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (for topping)
  • 1 tsp lemon juice (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Line a 24cm springform pan with parchment paper, including sides.
  2. Mix crushed biscuits, almonds, and melted butter. Press into pan and freeze.
  3. Beat cheeses and sugar. Add eggs one by one, mixing well each time.
  4. Add vanilla and lemon juice. Ensure smooth mixture.
  5. Place a tray with hot water at oven bottom for steam. Pour cheese mixture on crust and bake ~60 minutes until edges are set but center jiggles.
  6. Mix topping ingredients. Pour on cheesecake and bake 10 more minutes.
  7. Cool to room temp, then refrigerate overnight. Optional: dust edges with nougat powder before serving.
